PIZZA DOUGH RECIPE

Fresh and delicious pizza dough for your entire family

Thin Crust Pizza dough

Sure! To save time, you can make this thin crust pizza dough about five days in advance. stored it in an airtight storage container. Allow the refrigerated dough to sit for about 30 minutes at room temp before using.
Ingredients
  • 1 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1/4 tsp white sugard
  • 3/4 cup lukewarm water
  • 2 cups all purpose flower divided
  • 1/2 tsp salt
Steps to make
  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Disolve yeast and sugar in warm water thats 110 degrees F in a small bowl. Let stand until yeast softens and begins to form a creamy foam.
  3. Combine 1 3/4 cups flour and salt in a large bowl. Pour in yeast mixtur; mix well until dough forms.
  4. Knead dough on floured surface until smooth, about 2 mins
  5. Roll Dough into a 12-inch circle. Transfer to greased pan
  6. Add your favorite toppings and bake
