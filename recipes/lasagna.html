Lasagna

Bake up this amamzing lasagna to impress friends and family alike! Easy to make and easier to enjoy, don't miss out on it!
Ingredients
  • 1 pound sweet italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans canned tomatoe sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tbsps white sugar
  • 4 tbsps dried basil
  • 1 1/2 tsps salt
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 12 Lasagna noodles
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated parm cheese
Steps to make
  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Cook meats, onion, and garlic in dutch oven over medium heat
  3. Stir in tomatoe sauce, paste and add water. Season with sugar, parsley, basil, 1 tsp salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds and pepper. Simmer, covered for 1 1/2 hours stirring occasionally.
  4. bring pot of salt water to boil. Cook Lasagna noodles for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain Noodles and rinse with cold water.
  5. in a bowl combine cheese with egg and parsley add a punch of salt.
  6. Preheat overn to 375 degrees F
  7. spread meat suace in bottom of pan and arrange six noodles over meat sauce overlapping slightly. Spread 1/2 cheese mixture, top with cheese slices Spoon meat suace over cheese and sprinkle parmesan cheese.
  8. Repeat layers and top with remaining cheese. Cover with foil.
  9. Bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake additional 25 minutes.
  10. Rest lasagna 15 minutes before serving.
